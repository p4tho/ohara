# Ohara
Research papers provide a lot of information and is primarily digested through reading. Podcasts from conversations, audiobooks from books, radio from newspapers are examples of how absorbing information has changed. Ohara adds research papers to the pile by being an all-in-one multiplatform app that includes a .pdf to .m4b converter and M4B player. This project was named after the island that was home to the Tree of Knowledge and scholars who could read the Poneglyphs.

## Robin
Ohara requires .m4b files to track timestamps of each section of a research paper. Robin's job is to translate files from .pdf to .m4b by parsing the text under each .pdf bookmark.
